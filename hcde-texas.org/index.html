HCDE Tax Rate

HCDE saves taxpayers money with lowered tax rate  

 

Harris County Department of Education is committed to being good stewards of taxpayer dollars. For the fifth year in a row, HCDE's Board of Trustees voted to save county taxpayers money by lowering the tax rate below the effective tax rate.

HCDEs adopted tax rate is 0.005000 per $100 assessed property value for tax year 2018-2019. For the average home in Harris County, valued at $167,000, this equates to $8.35 a year.

Learn more and calculate your tax rate.
