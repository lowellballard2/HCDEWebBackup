English Language Arts and Social Studies

We provide customized training and conferences at HCDE or at your location for teachers, administrators and support staff through the Teaching and Learning Center. Our content area specialists for English Language Arts (ELA) and Social Studies are experienced educators who give teachers the training and professional development needed to support academic achievement.

On this page you'll find a topic and class list of ELA and Social Studies courses, course registration links, and contact information for additional questions or issues.

English Language Arts (ELA)

We offer relevant, responsive, and research-based professional learning that results in increased student achievement and teacher engagement. This includes a wide variety of courses in topics specifically designed to improve and further develop your Professional ELA  and Social Studies abilities.

Current or previously offered ELA courses include:

  •  6 +1 Traits © National Workshops
  •  Deep Dive in the New TEKS for ELAR and SLAR
  •  STAAR and EOC examinations of released
  •  District-Wide Portfolio Systems Traditional and Digital
  •  Connecting TEKS to Quality Assessment
  •  Creating a Successful EOC Re-Tester Class for English EOC I & II
  •  Mentor Text training for all grade levels
  •  Guided Reading training for elementary
  •  What Successful Readers and Writers Do-Elementary and Secondary
  •  Build Your Bookshelf-What Books to read next-elementary and secondary
  •  English language arts Leadership Group Meetings

Campuses can contract for any/all of the following services:

  •  Academic Coaching
  •  STAAR/EOC Success Strategies for grades 1-3
  •  Starting and Maintaining Successful PLC’s
  •  Language Arts/Literacy Teacher Training for Grades 6-12

Social Studies

Current or previously offered Social Studies courses include:

  •  TEKS/STAAR Analysis
  •  Writing Social Studies STAAR Items for Classroom Use
  •  Content Writing in Social Studies (9-12)
  •  Using Children's Literature to Teach Social Studies (K-5)
  •  Strategies for World Cultures and World Geography




  •  Social Studies Leadership Group Meetings
  •  Project Based Learning--How to Implement
  •  Applying Problem Solving Strategies to Social Studies Content
  •  Classroom Instructional Support
  •  Social Studies Educator Professional Development  

Not seeing what you're looking for? Contact us and we can build custom courses to meet your needs!

Upcoming Workshops

Ready to check out our upcoming workshops? Our courses change throughout the year depending on the needs of teachers, administrators, and support staff. Currently our workshops are performed on a virtual platform through September 2020. Please ensure to check back as time progresses for any changes to time, dates, and/or locations of courses.

Each of the listed courses below directly link to the registration page. Once registered, you will receive a confirmation email with the course details. Should you need to cancel, please contact Liliana Maldonado. For additional course questions, please contact the listed instructor. For general Teaching and Learning questions, check out our Staff Contact page.

We look forward to seeing you!
